Funding according to ¬ß81 SGB III (BGS) or continuing education in the company or QCG

Goal: Teaching of knowledge and skills in the socio-pedagogical and care sector for work with children and young people who need individual support in (school) everyday life.

Target groups:

  • Employees subject to social insurance contributions without relevant socio-pedagogical or pedagogical training, who carry out an activity as school assistance according to ¬ß112 SGB IX or as school accompaniment according to ¬ß35a SGB VIII

Start: 25.09.2023, End: 27.09.2024, individual entry possible

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to important questions

How does AVGS funding work?

+

AVGS (Activation and Placement Voucher) is issued by the German Employment Agency or Job Center. It covers 100% of career coaching costs. We support you with the application process and explain all requirements.

Is my data safe during coaching sessions?

+

100% GDPR compliant. All conversations are confidential. Your data stays in Germany on secure servers. You retain full control - no data sharing without your explicit consent.

What results can I expect from coaching?

+

It depends on your situation and commitment. Some clients find clarity quickly, others need time for implementation. We can't promise miracles, but we give you honest assessment of what's realistic for your specific situation.

Do you only work with German clients?

+

AVGS and BAFA funding are only available in Germany. However, we offer private coaching internationally in German, English, and Spanish. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What happens after the initial consultation?

+

You receive a realistic assessment of your possibilities and an honest plan for next steps. No exaggerated promises, but solid foundation for your decisions. Together we decide if and how to proceed.
